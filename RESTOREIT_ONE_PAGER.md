# restoreit — Product Overview

**Cloud-based forensic file restoration that never writes to your drive.**

[restoreit.app](https://restoreit.app)

---

## The Problem

When you lose files, every piece of advice on the internet tells you to install recovery software.

That's the worst thing you can do.

Installing software onto the same drive that lost your data writes new data to the disk — potentially overwriting the very files you're trying to save. The more you use the drive, the less recoverable your data becomes.

---

## The Solution

**restoreit scans your drive without writing a single byte.**

A lightweight relay script runs entirely in memory. It reads your drive's raw sectors and streams them to restoreit's cloud forensics engine. The engine reconstructs your files and stores them securely in restoreit Cloud.

You see what's recoverable before you pay. If nothing is found, you're not charged.

| Step | What Happens |
